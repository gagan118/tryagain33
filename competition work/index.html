SHREE SHANTI NAMUNA

SECONDARY SCHOOL

Shree Shanti Namuna Secondary School is a prestigious educational institution committed to providing quality education
and fostering holistic development among its students. Established with the vision of nurturing young minds,
the school offers a comprehensive curriculum that balances academics, extracurricular activities, and character building. With
a team of dedicated educators and modern facilities, Shree Shanti Namuna Secondary
School aims to create a supportive and inspiring learning environment where students can thrive and achieve their full potential.

FACILITIES IN OUR SCHOOL

1. Well-equipped classrooms with modern teaching aids.
2. Science and computer laboratories for practical learning.
3. A well-stocked library with a diverse collection of books.
4. Sports facilities including a playground and indoor games.
5. Arts and music rooms to encourage creativity.
6. Canteen providing healthy meals and snacks.
7. Safe and secure campus with CCTV surveillance.
8. Transportation services for students' convenience.
9. Extracurricular clubs and activities for holistic development.

DEPARTMENTS IN OUR SCHOOL

1. Science Department: Focuses on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

The Science Department of Classes 11 and 12 at Shanti School, Tillottama-5, Manigram is well organized and focused
on quality education. The department offers science streams with subjects such as Physics, Chemistry,
Biology, and Mat hematics. It has qualified and experienced teachers who explain concepts clearly and
support students in practical learning.

2. Management Department: Specializes in Business Studies, Accountancy, and Economics.

The Management Department at Shanti School, Tillottama-5, Manigram offers a comprehensive curriculum for Classes 11 and 12.
It includes subjects like Business Studies, Accountancy, and Economics. The department is staffed with experienced teachers
who provide practical insights into the business world and help students develop essential management skills.

3. Hotel management and cooking department .

The Hotel Management and DHM Department of Shanti School provides both theoretical knowledge and practical
training to students. Subjects such as food production, food and beverage service, housekeeping, front office management,
and tourism studies are taught.
